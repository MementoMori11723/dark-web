This hidden service was created to provide a space where people can access information and services without fear of surveillance, censorship, or profiling.

Why It Exists

The internet is no longer a free space. Mass surveillance, censorship laws, and data exploitation have made privacy a privilege rather than a right. This project aims to reclaim some of that freedom by operating within the Tor network.

Core Principles

  1. Freedom of Access: Anyone with Tor can visit — there is no geoblocking or censorship here.
  2. Data Minimalism: We collect as little data as possible — ideally none.
  3. Security by Design: No trackers. No third-party scripts. No leaks.
  4. Decentralization Friendly: We support projects like IPFS, Tails, and Monero.
"In a world where surveillance is the norm, privacy becomes resistance."

We Recommend

  • Use Tails OS if your life depends on anonymity.
  • Enable JavaScript blocking if you’re unsure of the site’s safety.
  • Verify any .onion addresses through trusted channels.
